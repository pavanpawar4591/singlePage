Manufacturing facilities

Our 3 manufacturing units cover more than 40,000 m2 and are equipped with the latest technology, enabling us to offer solutions for any scale and with the highest precision

Each unit has state of the art machinery for fabrication, machining & quality control. Some examples of our machinery are CNC Boring machines, CNC gas cutting, Stress relieving furnace, Hydraulic press, Plate bending machine, and Leica Absolute Tracker. Additionally, a wide range of NDT are performed in-house.

We are one of the few companies in India to have such high grade capabilities. We are continuously upgrading them to complete all our projects successfully and to the satisfaction of our clients.

Careers

At Kay Bouvet we want to keep pushing the limits of what can be achieved by heavy engineering.

Our ambition is to redefine the Indian Nuclear, Defence & Space technological landscape in the years to come and leverage our sugar expertise in new markets worldwide . This is a challenging task, which is why we are constantly looking for new talented individuals to help us realize our vision.

Kay Bouvet is the perfect place for you if you are:
- A young professional looking for a fast-paced career in one of the fastest growing industries in the world
- A proactive person who excels in a team environment
- Someone willing to be responsible for making great projects become a reality from your first day in our company
- Someone constantly looking to find innovative solutions to engineering problems At Kay Bouvet you will find continuous learning opportunities, a team of professionals passionate about making a difference in their respective fields. You will be supported by a management team which has gained experience from some of the top organizations in the world, like London Business School, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, and McKinsey&Company, and is looking for new ideas to redefine the way heavy manufacturing is done today.
